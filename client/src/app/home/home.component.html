This project is the result of requirements for both CS290 and CS340.

Some features of this website:

  • Create an account as either an author or a reader
  • Search for books, authors and readers
  • Sort the results by various properties such as Rating or Year
  • Post a review to any book
  • Add a new book and mark other authors as joint authors
  • Check out profiles of other readers
